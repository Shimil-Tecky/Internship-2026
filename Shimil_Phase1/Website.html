Iphone

The iPhone is a line of smartphones developed by Apple Inc. that runs the iOS operating system. Since its introduction by Steve Jobs in 2007, it has revolutionized the mobile industry by popularizing the touch-screen interface.

Key Aspects of the iPhone:

  • Design & Hardware: iPhones are known for high-quality, durable design, with recent models featuring Ceramic Shield glass and USB-C connectivity.
  • Operating System (iOS): Renowned for its user-friendly interface, security, and smooth performance.
  • Security & Privacy: Apple emphasizes data protection and privacy, with apps vetted for the App Store.
